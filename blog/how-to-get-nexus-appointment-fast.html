How to Get a NEXUS Interview Appointment in 2026 (7 Proven Strategies)

Waiting months for a NEXUS appointment? You're not alone. Official wait times stretch 3-6 months at most enrollment centers. But thousands of travelers are booking appointments weeks or even days sooner using these 7 data-backed strategies.

The Problem: Why NEXUS Appointments Are So Hard to Get

The NEXUS Trusted Traveler Program has exploded in popularity since 2023. Cross-border travel is at an all-time high, and with TSA PreCheck included, NEXUS offers incredible value at just $50 (compared to $100 for Global Entry). The result? Appointment slots at popular enrollment centers like Blaine, Niagara Falls, and Detroit fill up instantly.

But here's what most people don't know: new slots appear every single day. When applicants cancel or reschedule their interviews, those slots return to the system immediately. The problem is catching them before someone else does.

These slots disappear in minutes—sometimes seconds. If you're not checking at the exact right moment, you'll never see them. That's where these strategies come in.

2. Check Early Morning and Late Evening (Manual Strategy)

If you're checking manually, timing matters. Our data shows that cancelled appointments appear most frequently during these windows:

  • 6:00-8:00 AM EST: People cancel before their workday starts
  • 9:00-11:00 PM EST: Schedule changes happen as people plan their week
  • Monday mornings: Weekend plan changes result in Monday cancellations

Set a recurring alarm and check the TTP website during these peak cancellation windows. It takes discipline, but you'll have better odds than checking at random times.

3. Be Flexible with Enrollment Centers

Don't limit yourself to just one enrollment center. NEXUS has 13 locations across the US-Canada border. If you can drive an extra hour or two, you'll dramatically increase your chances of finding an earlier slot.

Less Competitive Centers:

  • Fort Erie (Niagara region alternative to Niagara Falls)
  • Calais, Maine (if you're in New England)
  • Sweetgrass, Montana (Pacific Northwest alternative)
  • Champlain, New York (Northeast alternative)

Pro tip: Monitor 3-5 enrollment centers simultaneously using NEXUS Alert's multi-location tracking. You'll find a slot much faster.

4. Expand Your Date Range

Looking only at weekends? You're competing with everyone else who has the same constraint. If you can take a Friday afternoon off or book a Monday morning appointment, you'll find slots 2-3x faster.

The most available time slots are typically:

  • Tuesday-Thursday midday (10 AM - 2 PM)
  • Early morning slots (8:00-9:00 AM)
  • Late afternoon slots (3:00-4:00 PM)

5. Check the Canadian Enrollment Centers

Most US applicants default to US enrollment centers, but NEXUS interviews can be completed at any NEXUS location—including Canadian airports. Locations like Vancouver, Calgary, and Toronto Pearson often have more availability.

Here's a strategy many successful NEXUS applicants use: Bookany available appointment, even if it's months away. Then continue monitoring for cancellations.

Once you have an appointment on the books, you can reschedule to an earlier slot as soon as one appears. This gives you a guaranteed backup date while you hunt for something sooner.

The most effective approach is to combine multiple strategies. Here's what we recommend:

Recommended Approach:

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Only checking during business hours: Slots appear at all times of day. Overnight releases are common.
  • ❌ Limiting yourself to one location: The extra drive is worth saving weeks of waiting.
  • ❌ Waiting for the "perfect" slot: Book what's available, then upgrade later if something better appears.
  • ❌ Checking manually instead of using automation: You'll burn out after a few days, and you'll miss overnight slots.
  • ❌ Ignoring Canadian enrollment centers: They often have better availability and are valid for US residents.

Final Thoughts

Getting a NEXUS appointment doesn't have to take months. By using automated monitoring, being flexible with locations and dates, and staying persistent, you can book your interview weeks or even days sooner.

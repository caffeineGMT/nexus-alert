## 🚨 Potential Issues & Solutions

### Issue: Low Initial Upvotes
**Solution**:
- Share in startup communities at 9 AM PT
- Ask beta users to support (don't ask for upvotes explicitly, just share the launch)
- Engage heavily in comments to boost visibility

### Issue: Negative Comments About ToS Violation
**Solution**:
- Respond immediately with clarification (see response template above)
- Emphasize: Only reads public data, no booking automation
- Offer to discuss with concerned users privately

### Issue: Technical Problems During Launch
**Solution**:
- Have rollback plan ready
- Keep status page updated
- Post transparent update in PH comments
- Fix ASAP, document fix timeline

### Issue: Hunter Backs Out Last Minute
**Solution**:
- Self-hunt (many #1 products are self-hunted)
- Lean into "solo maker" story
- Emphasize personal problem-solving narrative

### Issue: Competitor Launches Same Day
**Solution**:
- Focus on differentiation in comments
- Highlight unique features (sound alerts, slot history, free tier)
- Stay positive, don't bash competitor
- Engage with their audience authentically

## 🎯 Key Decisions Made

### 1. Long, Keyword-Rich Title
**Decision:** Use 74-character title with exact-match keywords vs. short brand name
**Reasoning:** Chrome Web Store search heavily weights title keywords
**Trade-off:** Slightly less memorable, but 10x better for SEO
**Expected Impact:** Move from "not ranked" to #1-3 for primary keyword

### 2. Review Acquisition Incentive
**Decision:** Offer 1 free month of Premium ($4.99) for 5-star reviews
**Reasoning:** 50+ reviews by Day 30 is aggressive without incentive
**Cost:** ~$500 (100 reviewers × $4.99)
**ROI:** $4,000-$6,000 additional annual revenue from improved rankings
**Justification:** 8x-12x return on investment

### 3. Front-Load Keywords in Descriptions
**Decision:** Put "NEXUS appointment finder" in first 20 characters
**Reasoning:** Chrome Web Store algorithm prioritizes early keyword placement
**Expected Impact:** 40-60% higher click-through rate from search results

### 4. Screenshot Caption Optimization
**Decision:** Include target keywords naturally in all 5 captions
**Reasoning:** Captions are indexed by search. Competitors don't optimize this.
**Expected Impact:** 10-20% ranking boost (underutilized ranking factor)

### 5. 30-Day Aggressive Timeline
**Decision:** Target #1 ranking by Day 30 (vs. conservative 60-90 day timeline)
**Reasoning:** Launch window is critical. Competitors are weak (low review counts).
**Risk:** May take 45-60 days instead. Still worth aggressive push.
**Mitigation:** Weekly tracking + A/B testing + multi-channel promotion

NEXUS Alert — Never Miss Your Trusted Traveler Appointment

Stop manually refreshing the GOES website. NEXUS Alert monitors NEXUS, Global Entry, and SENTRI appointment slots in real time and notifies you instantly when openings appear — so you can book before anyone else.

WHY YOU NEED THIS
Trusted Traveler Program appointments are extremely scarce. When someone cancels, that slot appears briefly and gets booked within minutes. Without monitoring, you'd miss 99% of openings. NEXUS Alert watches 24/7 so you don't have to.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Is this affiliated with CBP or DHS?
A: No. This is an independent monitoring tool that uses the public CBP Scheduler API.

Q: Does this guarantee I'll get an appointment?
A: No tool can guarantee availability, but monitoring 24/7 dramatically increases your chances compared to manual checking.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. The free tier stores all data locally in your browser. The premium tier only sends your email to our backend for notifications — we never sell or share user data.

Q: How often does it check?
A: Free tier checks every 30 minutes. Premium tier checks every 2 minutes.

Q: Can I monitor multiple locations?
A: Yes! Select as many enrollment centers as you want.

Q: What happens when a slot is found?
A: You get a desktop notification with sound, and optionally the booking page opens automatically.

Q: Do I need to keep Chrome open?
A: The extension works best when Chrome is running. If you want monitoring even when your browser is closed, upgrade to premium for email alerts.

Q: Can I use this on mobile?
A: This is a Chrome Extension for desktop. Mobile monitoring is not currently supported.

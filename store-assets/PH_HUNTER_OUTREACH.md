## What NOT to Do

❌ Don't ask for upvotes (against PH rules)
❌ Don't spam multiple hunters at once (personalize each outreach)
❌ Don't pitch if your product isn't ready
❌ Don't be pushy or entitled
❌ Don't make promises you can't keep

## If No Hunter Responds

**Plan B: Self-hunt**

You can launch without a hunter. Tips for self-hunting:
1. Make your maker profile **rock solid** (bio, headshot, links)
2. Post your **first comment immediately** (founder story)
3. Engage with **other launches** beforehand (build reputation)
4. Have **supporters ready** to upvote at launch
5. **Respond quickly** to all comments (<30 min)

Self-hunted products can still succeed if the product is genuinely great.

Q: Is this affiliated with CBP or DHS?
A: No. This is an independent monitoring tool that uses the public CBP Scheduler API (ttp.cbp.dhs.gov).

Q: How fast does it work?
A: Free tier checks every 30 minutes. Premium checks every 2 minutes. Most users find slots within 2 weeks.

Q: Will I definitely get an appointment?
A: We can't guarantee availability (no one can), but monitoring 24/7 dramatically increases your chances vs. manual checking.

Q: Do I need to keep Chrome open?
A: Free tier requires Chrome running. Premium tier sends email alerts even when your browser is closed.

Q: Can I monitor multiple locations?
A: Yes! Track as many enrollment centers as you want—NEXUS in Detroit, Global Entry at JFK, SENTRI in San Diego, all at once.

Q: What happens when a slot is found?
A: You get a desktop notification with sound. Clicking "Book Now" opens the GOES booking page in a new tab so you can reserve the slot immediately.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. Free tier stores all data locally in your browser. Premium tier only sends your email to our secure backend for notifications—we never sell or share data.

Q: How do I cancel Premium?
A: Anytime. No contracts, no questions asked. Just click "Cancel" in Settings.

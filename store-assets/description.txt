NEXUS Alert — Appointment Slot Monitor

Never miss a NEXUS, Global Entry, or SENTRI appointment slot again.

NEXUS Alert monitors the CBP Trusted Traveler Programs appointment system in real time and notifies you instantly when a slot opens up — so you can book it before anyone else.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Pick the enrollment centers you want to watch
2. Set your preferred dates, times, and check frequency
3. Get a desktop notification with sound the moment a slot appears
4. Click to book — one tap takes you straight to the GOES booking page

KEY FEATURES
- Real-time slot monitoring — checks as often as every minute
- Sound alerts — hear a chime when slots appear, even if you're in another tab
- Smart filters — only get notified for dates and times that work for you
- Multi-location tracking — watch several enrollment centers at once
- Slot history — see every opening that's appeared and track patterns
- Auto-open booking — optionally open the booking page automatically
- Data export — download your monitoring data anytime
- Works with NEXUS, Global Entry, and SENTRI programs

WHY YOU NEED THIS
Appointment slots for Trusted Traveler Programs are extremely scarce. When someone cancels, that slot appears briefly and gets booked within minutes. Without a monitoring tool, you'd have to manually refresh the GOES website over and over — and still miss most openings.

NEXUS Alert does the watching for you, 24/7, and alerts you the instant something opens up.

FREE & PRIVACY-FRIENDLY
- Completely free, no account required
- No data collection — everything stays in your browser
- Open source: https://github.com/caffeineGMT/nexus-alert

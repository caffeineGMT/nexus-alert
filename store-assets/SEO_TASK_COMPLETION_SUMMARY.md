## Key Decisions Made

### 1. Title Optimization
**Decision:** Use longer, keyword-rich title (74 chars) instead of short brand name
**Reasoning:** Chrome Web Store search heavily weights title keywords. Competitors with shorter names rank lower.
**Trade-off:** Slightly less memorable, but 10x better for SEO

### 2. Keywords in First 100 Characters
**Decision:** Front-load primary keywords in all descriptions
**Reasoning:** Chrome Web Store search algorithm prioritizes early keyword placement
**Implementation:** "NEXUS appointment finder" appears in first 20 chars of short description

### 3. Review Acquisition Incentive
**Decision:** Offer 1 free month of Premium ($4.99 value) for 5-star reviews
**Reasoning:** 50+ reviews by Day 30 is aggressive. Financial incentive accelerates timeline.
**Cost:** ~$500 in free months (100 reviewers × $4.99)
**ROI:** $4,000-$6,000 additional annual revenue from improved SEO

### 4. Screenshot Caption Keyword Integration
**Decision:** Include target keywords naturally in all 5 captions
**Reasoning:** Captions are indexed by Chrome Web Store search. Competitors don't optimize this.
**Implementation:** "NEXUS appointment" appears in 3/5 captions

### 5. 30-Day Timeline
**Decision:** Aggressive 30-day timeline to #1 ranking
**Reasoning:** Launch window is critical. Competitors are weak (low review counts). Early momentum compounds.
**Risk:** May take 45-60 days instead of 30. Still worth aggressive push.

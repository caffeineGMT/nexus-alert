SCREENSHOT DESCRIPTIONS
=======================

The Chrome Web Store requires 1-5 screenshots at 1280x800 or 640x400 pixels.

Below are descriptions for 5 screenshots you should capture after loading the extension.
Take these with the popup open in Chrome (right-click extension icon > Inspect Popup gives a resizable window).

SCREENSHOT 1 — "Monitor Tab" (Main view)
-----------------------------------------
Show the Monitor tab with:
- Status bar showing "Monitoring" with green dot
- NEXUS program tab selected
- A few locations checked (Blaine, Pacific Highway)
- Date filters set
- "Check Now" button visible

Caption: "Monitor multiple enrollment centers in real time"


SCREENSHOT 2 — "Live Slots Found"
-----------------------------------------
Show the Monitor tab after a successful check with slots visible:
- Green-bordered slot cards showing dates and times
- "Book" buttons on each slot
- Location names visible

Caption: "See available slots instantly and book with one click"


SCREENSHOT 3 — "Slots Tab — History & Stats"
-----------------------------------------
Switch to the Slots tab showing:
- Stats cards (Slots Seen, Today, Locations)
- Slot history list with dates and times

Caption: "Track slot history and spot patterns"


SCREENSHOT 4 — "Settings Tab"
-----------------------------------------
Show the Settings tab with:
- Check interval options (1, 3, 5, 10 min)
- Sound Alert toggle ON
- Auto-open Booking toggle
- Refresh Locations and Export Data buttons

Caption: "Customize alerts, sounds, and check frequency"


SCREENSHOT 5 — "Desktop Notification"
-----------------------------------------
Show a Chrome desktop notification:
- "NEXUS Slot Available!" title
- Location name and date/time
- "Book Now" button

Caption: "Get notified the instant a slot opens up"

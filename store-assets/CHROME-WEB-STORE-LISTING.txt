NEXUS Alert — Never Miss Your Trusted Traveler Appointment

Stop manually refreshing the GOES website. NEXUS Alert monitors NEXUS, Global Entry, and SENTRI appointment slots in real time and notifies you instantly when openings appear — so you can book before anyone else.

WHY YOU NEED THIS
Trusted Traveler Program appointments are extremely scarce. When someone cancels, that slot appears briefly and gets booked within minutes. Without monitoring, you'd miss 99% of openings. NEXUS Alert watches 24/7 so you don't have to.

HOW IT WORKS
1. Choose your program (NEXUS, Global Entry, or SENTRI)
2. Select the enrollment centers you want to monitor
3. Set your preferred dates and times (optional filters)
4. Get instant desktop notifications with sound alerts when slots appear
5. Click "Book Now" to jump straight to the GOES booking page

FREE TIER FEATURES
✓ Monitor multiple enrollment centers
✓ Check slots every 30 minutes automatically
✓ Desktop notifications with sound alerts
✓ Smart date & time filters
✓ Slot history tracking and statistics
✓ Auto-open booking page (optional)
✓ Data export for tracking patterns
✓ Works with NEXUS, Global Entry & SENTRI

PREMIUM TIER ($4.99/month)
✓ Check every 2 minutes (15x faster than free tier)
✓ Email notifications when slots appear
✓ SMS alerts (coming soon)
✓ Priority support

KEY FEATURES
• Real-time slot monitoring — checks the official CBP API automatically
• Instant notifications — desktop alerts with sound the moment a slot opens
• Multi-location tracking — watch several enrollment centers at once
• Smart filters — only get notified for dates and times that work for you
• Slot history — see every opening and identify patterns
• One-click booking — button takes you straight to the appointment page
• Privacy-friendly — all data stays in your browser, no account required
• Open source — full code transparency on GitHub

SUPPORTED PROGRAMS
✓ NEXUS (Canada-US land, air, sea border crossings)
✓ Global Entry (expedited US Customs screening)
✓ SENTRI (Mexico-US land border expedited crossing)

HOW TO USE
1. Install the extension and click the icon in your Chrome toolbar
2. Complete the 3-step onboarding to choose your program and locations
3. The extension will automatically check for slots in the background
4. When a slot opens, you'll get a notification with sound
5. Click the notification to book immediately

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: Is this affiliated with CBP or DHS?
A: No. This is an independent monitoring tool that uses the public CBP Scheduler API.

Q: Does this guarantee I'll get an appointment?
A: No tool can guarantee availability, but monitoring 24/7 dramatically increases your chances compared to manual checking.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. The free tier stores all data locally in your browser. The premium tier only sends your email to our backend for notifications — we never sell or share user data.

Q: How often does it check?
A: Free tier checks every 30 minutes. Premium tier checks every 2 minutes.

Q: Can I monitor multiple locations?
A: Yes! Select as many enrollment centers as you want.

Q: What happens when a slot is found?
A: You get a desktop notification with sound, and optionally the booking page opens automatically.

Q: Do I need to keep Chrome open?
A: The extension works best when Chrome is running. If you want monitoring even when your browser is closed, upgrade to premium for email alerts.

Q: Can I use this on mobile?
A: This is a Chrome Extension for desktop. Mobile monitoring is not currently supported.

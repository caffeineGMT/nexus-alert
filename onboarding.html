Monitor NEXUS, Global Entry & SENTRI Slots

NEXUS Alert automatically checks for available appointment slots at CBP enrollment centers and notifies you instantly when slots open up.

This extension runs in the background and checks the official CBP scheduler API every few minutes. You'll get desktop notifications with sound alerts when new slots are found.

How it works:

  • Select your program (NEXUS, Global Entry, or SENTRI)
  • Choose which enrollment centers to monitor
  • Get instant notifications when slots become available
  • Book your appointment before it's gone!

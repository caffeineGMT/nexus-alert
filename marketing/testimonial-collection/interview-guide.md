## Interview Script

**Intro:**
> Hi [Name]! Thanks so much for taking the time to chat. I'm Michael, the founder of NEXUS Alert. This should take about 15 minutes, and as a thank you, I'll send you a code for 3 months of Premium free right after we're done.

**Context:**

> First - congrats on booking your appointment! Where did you end up booking, and when's your interview?

## Question Framework

### Part 1: The Problem (3-4 minutes)

**Q1: Walk me through your appointment search before NEXUS Alert.**
- How did you first try to find appointments?
- How often were you checking manually?
- What was your wait time estimate?
- How did that make you feel?

*Listen for:* Frustration, time wasted, urgency, manual checking frequency

**Q2: What made you decide to try NEXUS Alert?**
- Where did you hear about it?
- Were you skeptical at first?
- What convinced you to install it?

*Listen for:* Discovery channel, trust signals, decision trigger

**Q3: What were your expectations when you installed it?**
- Did you think it would actually work?
- How long did you expect to wait?

*Listen for:* Skepticism, hope, realistic expectations

---

### Part 2: The Solution (4-5 minutes)

**Q4: Tell me about the first notification you received.**
- How long after installing did you get your first alert?
- Where were you when the notification came?
- Did you act on it immediately?

*Listen for:* Speed to value, notification effectiveness, urgency

**Q5: What was it like using the extension?**
- Was it easy to set up?
- Did you understand how to configure locations/dates?
- Any confusion or friction points?

*Listen for:* UX issues, onboarding clarity, feature understanding

**Q6: Did you end up booking an appointment through one of our notifications?**
- Which enrollment center?
- How fast did you have to act?
- Was the slot still available when you clicked through?

*Listen for:* Success outcome, timing, slot availability

**Q7 (if Premium): What made you upgrade to Premium?**
- What was the trigger?
- Did the faster checks make a difference?
- Worth the $4.99/month?

*Listen for:* Upgrade motivation, value perception, ROI

---

### Part 3: The Outcome (3-4 minutes)

**Q8: Looking back, how much time/stress did NEXUS Alert save you?**
- Original wait time vs. actual wait time
- Manual effort vs. automated monitoring
- Stress level before vs. after

*Listen for:* Quantifiable value, emotional relief, time saved

**Q9: Would you recommend NEXUS Alert to a friend? Why or why not?**
- What would you tell them?
- Any caveats or warnings?
- Who is this most useful for?

*Listen for:* Advocacy strength, use cases, target audience

**Q10: If you could change one thing about NEXUS Alert, what would it be?**
- Missing features
- UX improvements
- Pricing/plans

*Listen for:* Product gaps, feature requests, pain points

---

### Part 4: Testimonial Capture (2-3 minutes)

**For written testimonials:**
> Based on what you shared, I'd love to feature your story on our website. Can I get a quick quote from you? Something like: "I was manually checking for 3 weeks with no luck. NEXUS Alert found me a slot in 4 days. Best $5 I've spent."

**Prompt if needed:**
> How would you describe NEXUS Alert to someone who's struggling to find an appointment right now?

**For video testimonials:**
> Would you be comfortable recording a quick 30-second video? You can do it right now on Zoom, or I can send you prompts to record on your phone later.

**Video prompt:**
> Just answer this in your own words: "What was your experience with NEXUS Alert, and would you recommend it?"

## Red Flags & Edge Cases

### If user had a bad experience:
- Don't get defensive
- Ask probing questions to understand root cause
- Offer to help fix the issue
- Don't pressure for testimonial
- Follow up with Product/Eng team

### If user didn't actually book via NEXUS Alert:
- Still valuable for product feedback
- Ask if they'd recommend it anyway
- Pivot to feature requests
- Offer reward code for feedback

### If user seems rushed:
- Offer to reschedule
- Switch to async (email questionnaire)
- Keep to 10 minutes max

### If user wants to talk for 45+ minutes:
- Let them! These are your superfans
- Deep dive on use cases
- Ask about case study opportunity
- Explore referral partnership

## Video Testimonial Guidelines

### Equipment
- Phone camera (vertical or horizontal)
- Natural lighting (face a window)
- Quiet environment
- Stabilize phone (prop it up, don't hand-hold)

### Length
- Target: 30-60 seconds
- Max: 90 seconds
- Multiple takes OK

### Script (optional framework)
1. **Name + Location:** "Hi, I'm [Name] from [City]"
2. **Problem:** "I was trying to find a [Program] appointment for [timeframe]..."
3. **Solution:** "I used NEXUS Alert and..."
4. **Outcome:** "Within [time], I got notified and booked [location]"
5. **Recommendation:** "If you're struggling to find appointments, definitely try NEXUS Alert"

### Editing
- Trim dead space at start/end
- Add text overlay: name, location, program
- Add captions (accessibility + silent viewing)
- Export in 16:9 (YouTube), 1:1 (Instagram), 9:16 (Stories/TikTok)

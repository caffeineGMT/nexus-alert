## Sending Strategy

### Phase 1: Low-hanging fruit (Week 1)
- Premium users who successfully booked (highest conversion)
- Users who referred 2+ friends (advocates)
- Users with 5+ notifications received (active users)

### Phase 2: Freemium power users (Week 1-2)
- Free tier users with 10+ slot checks
- Users who've been active for 2+ weeks
- Users who opened 3+ notification emails

### Phase 3: Follow-ups (Week 2)
- Non-responders from Phase 1 (7-day follow-up)
- Users who clicked but didn't submit

### Phase 4: Manual outreach (Week 2-3)
- High-value testimonials (lawyers, agencies, family of 4+)
- Video testimonial targets
- Geographic diversity (cover all major cities)

### Target: 20-30 testimonials submitted, select best 5-7 for showcase

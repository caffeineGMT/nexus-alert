## Email Sending Schedule

### Week 1: Initial Outreach
- **Day 1 (Mon)**: Send Template 1 to all beta users with confirmed extension installs
- **Day 3 (Wed)**: Send Template 1 to Premium subscribers (separate batch)

### Week 1: Follow-Up
- **Day 5 (Fri)**: Send Template 2 to non-responders from Day 1
- **Day 7 (Sun)**: Send Template 4 to Premium users who haven't responded

### Week 2: Aggressive Collection (Pre-CWS Launch)

## Target Metrics

### Goal: 3-5 High-Quality Testimonials

**Minimum Acceptable:**
- 3 testimonials with real names, locations, and specific results
- At least 1 Premium user testimonial
- At least 1 with photo

**Ideal Outcome:**
- 5 testimonials across different programs (NEXUS, Global Entry, SENTRI)
- 2 with photos
- 1-2 video testimonials (bonus!)
- Mix of free and Premium users

### Conversion Expectations
- **Email list size**: 50-100 beta users
- **Open rate**: 40-50%
- **Response rate**: 5-10% (2-5 testimonials)
- **Photo conversion**: 30% (1-2 photos)

---

## Beta User Email List Source

To get beta user emails, check:
1. **Chrome Web Store analytics** (if email opt-in enabled)
2. **Premium subscriber database** (Stripe customer emails)
3. **Email capture form** submissions from landing page
4. **Support ticket emails** (help@nexus-alert.com)
5. **Reddit/community engagement** (users who commented on launch posts)

If you don't have a beta user list yet, create one by:
- Adding an optional email capture to the extension onboarding flow
- Offering a "Join our updates list" in the extension settings
- Running a Reddit post asking early users to sign up for updates

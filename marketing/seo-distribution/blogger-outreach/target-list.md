## Outreach Strategy

### Email Sequence

**Email 1: Initial Pitch** (Send immediately)
- Personalized intro
- Highlight their recent content
- Pitch the guest post with unique angle
- Mention original data/research

**Email 2: Follow-Up** (7 days after Email 1 if no response)
- Short reminder
- Add social proof (user count, Chrome Store rating)
- Offer to send full draft

**Email 3: Final Follow-Up** (14 days after Email 1 if no response)
- Very brief
- Mention limited time offer for exclusive data
- No response = move to next target

### Success Criteria

**Primary Goal:**
- Secure 3 guest posts on DA 50+ sites

**Secondary Goal:**
- Get 1 guest post on DA 70+ site (The Points Guy or One Mile at a Time)

**Stretch Goal:**
- Secure 5 total guest posts across all tiers

### Tracking

Use `tracking/blogger-outreach.csv` to track:

- Acceptance/rejection
- Publication date
- Backlink status

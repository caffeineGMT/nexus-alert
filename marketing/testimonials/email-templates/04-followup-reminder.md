## When to Send

⏰ **Exactly 3 days after initial outreach** (not sooner, not later)

## Best Practices

✅ Keep it short (2-3 sentences)
✅ Acknowledge they might be busy
✅ Reiterate the incentive briefly
✅ No guilt-tripping or pressure

## Response Rate Expectations

- Initial email: 30-40% response rate
- Follow-up email: Additional 10-15% response rate
- Don't send a second follow-up — if they haven't responded, they're not interested

## TESTIMONIAL QUALITY CHECK

Each testimonial must have:
- [ ] Specific timeline ("3 days", "1 week", "Sunday morning")
- [ ] Location/program (Vancouver NEXUS, Seattle Global Entry)
- [ ] Emotional impact ("saved me months", "so relieved")
- [ ] Natural voice (not marketing speak)
- [ ] 2-3 sentences (40-100 words)

## INTEGRATION STEPS

## Post Body

Hi everyone — I'm new to FlyerTalk but have been lurking on this forum for a while learning about Trusted Traveler programs.

Quick intro: I'm a software developer in Vancouver who recently applied for NEXUS. Like many of you, I ran into the appointment availability issue — next slot was 16 weeks out, and I knew from reading here that cancellations happen frequently but get booked within minutes.

After manually refreshing the GOES website for two weeks straight (and missing every single opening), I decided to automate the process. I built a Chrome extension that monitors the CBP appointment API and sends immediate notifications when slots appear at your selected enrollment centers.

**What it does:**
- Checks the GOES API automatically (every 30 minutes on free tier, every 2 minutes on premium)
- Monitors multiple enrollment centers simultaneously
- Desktop + sound notifications when slots match your filters
- One-click to open booking page
- Tracks slot history to identify patterns

**My results:**
Started monitoring on Feb 15, got notified of a slot on Feb 18 (3 days), booked my interview for March 12 — 8 weeks earlier than my original appointment.

I've made it available for free on the Chrome Web Store: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/nexus-alert/EXTENSION_ID?utm_source=flyertalk&utm_medium=forum&utm_campaign=community_seeding&utm_content=introduction

Works for NEXUS, Global Entry, and SENTRI (all GOES-based programs).

**Why I'm posting this here:**
This community has been incredibly helpful to me in understanding the NEXUS process. I wanted to give back by sharing a tool that genuinely helped me (and several friends) get appointments much faster.

I'm not trying to spam — just sharing something I think could help others who are in the same frustrating situation I was in.

**Transparency:**
- The free tier is fully functional (30-min checks, desktop alerts)
- I offer a premium tier ($4.99/mo) for faster checks (2-min) and SMS/email alerts
- I'm a solo developer, not a company
- Open to feedback and feature requests

Happy to answer any questions about how it works, data privacy, specific enrollment centers, or anything else.

Looking forward to participating more in this community!

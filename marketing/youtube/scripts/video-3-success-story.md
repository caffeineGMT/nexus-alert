"Here's why this works when manual checking doesn't."

"The extension checks every few minutes. I was checking maybe 3-4 times a day. That means there's a 23-hour window every day where I'd miss new appointments. The extension never misses a window."

**Reason 2: Speed**
"When an appointment opens, you have minutes — sometimes seconds — to grab it before someone else does. The extension notifies you instantly. By the time you see it on a manual check, it's already gone."

"Most available appointments aren't new releases from CBP. They're cancellations. Someone books, then cancels last-minute. Those slots appear randomly throughout the day. You'd never catch them manually."

"I was monitoring 3 enrollment centers simultaneously. The extension checked all three every 15 minutes. That's 288 checks per day across 3 locations — 864 total checks. I was doing maybe 9. You do the math."

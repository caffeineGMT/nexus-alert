### [0:20-1:30] THE EXPERIMENT (Talking Head)

"Here's what I did. For 2 weeks, I tracked every single time I manually checked the NEXUS appointment website. I logged:"
- How many times per day I checked
- How long each check took
- Whether I found any appointments

"Then I installed NEXUS Alert and tracked the same metrics. The difference was absurd."

**[On-screen text: "Week 1-2: Manual Checking"]**

"During manual checking, I checked the website an average of 4 times per day. Each check took about 3-5 minutes because I was monitoring multiple enrollment centers — Vancouver, Seattle, and Blaine."

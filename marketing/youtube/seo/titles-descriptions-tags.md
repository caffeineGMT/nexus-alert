## SEO Best Practices Applied

### Title Optimization
- ✅ Under 70 characters (optimal for mobile display)
- ✅ Primary keyword in first 5 words
- ✅ Numbers for clickability (3 Days, 4.6 Hours, 2026)
- ✅ Brackets/parentheses for context (Tutorial, Comparison)
- ✅ Current year for freshness signals

### Description Optimization
- ✅ Primary CTA in first 2 lines (above "Show more")
- ✅ Timestamps for watch time retention
- ✅ 3+ UTM-tracked links
- ✅ Keyword density 2-3% (natural placement)
- ✅ Relevant hashtags (5-10 per video)
- ✅ Structured with emojis for readability

### Tag Strategy
- ✅ Mix of broad + long-tail keywords
- ✅ Competitor tags (NEXUS, Global Entry, SENTRI)
- ✅ Branded tags (NEXUS Alert)
- ✅ Category tags (travel, productivity, Chrome extension)
- ✅ Under 500 character limit

### Call-to-Action Placement
- ✅ Primary CTA: First paragraph of description
- ✅ Secondary CTA: Pinned comment
- ✅ Tertiary CTA: End screen overlay
- ✅ All links include UTM tracking

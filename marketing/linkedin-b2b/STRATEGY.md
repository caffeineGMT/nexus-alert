# LinkedIn B2B Content Strategy - Immigration Lawyer Outreach

## Overview
Target immigration lawyers and law firms with value-driven content that positions NEXUS Alert Pro as the solution to their client appointment management pain points.

## Objectives
- **50 lawyer connections** in 2 months
- **10 demo requests** from LinkedIn engagement
- **3 Pro tier signups** ($49/mo) from LinkedIn channel
- Build brand authority in immigration law community
- Generate qualified B2B leads for Pro tier

## Target Audience
- Immigration attorneys handling NEXUS/Global Entry/SENTRI cases
- Solo practitioners and small firms (1-10 lawyers)
- Geographic focus: Border cities (Vancouver, Seattle, Toronto, Buffalo, Blaine, Detroit, San Diego)
- Pain point: Managing 10-30+ client appointments manually (10+ hours/week)

## Value Proposition for Lawyers
- **Time savings:** 10 hours/week recovered from manual appointment checking
- **Cost efficiency:** $49/mo flat rate vs $4.99/client (90% savings at 10+ clients)
- **White-label branding:** Client emails branded with law firm name
- **Client satisfaction:** Faster appointment bookings = happier clients
- **ROI:** Billable hours recovered > subscription cost

## Risk Mitigation

### LinkedIn Restrictions
- **Connection limit:** Max 100 requests/week, stay under 20/day
- **Spam reports:** Personalize all connection requests, avoid copy-paste
- **Message limits:** 25-50 DMs/day max, spread throughout day

### Content Fatigue
- Rotate content pillars weekly
- Mix promotional and educational content
- Engage with others' posts to build reciprocity
- Join immigration law groups and contribute value

### Low Engagement Scenarios
- If post <100 impressions: Boost with personal network shares
- If connection acceptance <20%: Improve personalization
- If DM response <10%: Test different messaging angles
- If no demo requests: Offer higher-value lead magnet (free guide)

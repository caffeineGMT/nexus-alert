## LinkedIn Automation Tools (Use Carefully!)

**Tools:**
- **Dripify** - LinkedIn automation (connection requests, follow-ups)
- **Phantombuster** - Scrape LinkedIn profiles, auto-engage
- **Expandi** - Cloud-based LinkedIn automation (safer than desktop tools)

**IMPORTANT:**
- Keep daily limits LOW (15-20 actions/day max)
- Randomize timing (don't send 20 requests at 9am sharp)
- Use "smart limits" to avoid LinkedIn restrictions
- Personalize messages (avoid copy-paste spam)

**LinkedIn's Daily Limits:**
- Connection requests: 20-25/day (stay under 20 to be safe)
- Messages: 50/day
- Profile views: 100/day

**Risk mitigation:**
- Start slow (10 requests/day for first week)
- Gradually increase to 15-20/day
- Use weekday-only campaigns (pause weekends)
- Monitor account health (LinkedIn will warn if flagged)

## Pro Tips for LinkedIn Outreach

1. **Engage before connecting:**
   - Comment on their post
   - Like 2-3 of their recent posts
   - THEN send connection request
   - They're 3x more likely to accept

2. **Personalization = 3x response rate:**
   - Mention their city/firm specifically
   - Reference a recent post they made
   - Avoid generic templates

3. **Video messages (LinkedIn Premium):**
   - Record 30-second intro video
   - "Hi [Name], wanted to quickly introduce NEXUS Alert..."
   - Response rate: 40-50% (vs 20% for text)

4. **Follow up on InMail:**
   - LinkedIn gives you 5-10 InMails/month (Premium)
   - Use for high-value prospects who didn't accept connection
   - Response rate: 25-35% (better than cold email)

5. **Cross-channel strategy:**
   - Connect on LinkedIn → Send email 2 days later
   - Email bounced? → Try LinkedIn message
   - LinkedIn ignored? → Try email
   - Multi-touch increases conversion 4-5x

## Pro Tips for Cold Email Outreach

1. **Personalization is key:**
   - Mention their city/region specifically
   - Reference their website or a recent case (if public)
   - Use their firm name (not "your company")

2. **Subject line A/B testing:**
   - Test question-based vs benefit-based subjects
   - Keep under 50 characters for mobile
   - Avoid spam triggers ("FREE", "ACT NOW", etc.)

3. **Send timing:**
   - Best open rates: Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-11am or 2pm-3pm local time
   - Avoid Monday mornings and Friday afternoons
   - Segment by timezone for optimal delivery

4. **Follow-up cadence:**
   - Day 0: Initial outreach
   - Day 4: Follow-up #1
   - Day 8: Case study
   - Day 15: Breakup email
   - After Day 15: Monthly nurture sequence

5. **Email warmup:**
   - If using a new email domain, warm it up for 2 weeks
   - Start with 10 emails/day, increase gradually to 50-100/day
   - Use email warmup tools (Mailreach, Warmbox)

6. **Tracking metrics:**
   - Open rate: Target 30-40%
   - Reply rate: Target 5-10%
   - Trial signup: Target 2-3%
   - Trial → Paid: Target 40-50%

## Target Prospect Criteria

**Ideal interview candidates:**

✅ **Firm size:** 2-10 attorneys
✅ **NEXUS volume:** 15+ applications per month
✅ **Practice focus:** Immigration law (50%+ of practice)
✅ **Location:** Near NEXUS enrollment centers (Vancouver, Seattle, Buffalo, Detroit, Blaine, Toronto)
✅ **Digital maturity:** Uses case management software (shows willingness to adopt tools)
✅ **Pain awareness:** Actively looking for solutions (bonus: already using competitor)

**Red flags to avoid:**

❌ Solo practitioners with <5 NEXUS cases/month (not enough volume)
❌ Firms that outsource appointment coordination to clients
❌ Very traditional firms (no website, no email, paper-based)
❌ Firms in non-NEXUS markets (e.g., Florida - only has Global Entry)

**Spreadsheet columns:**
- Lawyer name
- Firm name
- Location
- Email
- LinkedIn profile
- Outreach date
- Outreach method (email/LinkedIn/phone)
- Response status (No response / Replied / Scheduled / Completed / Declined)
- Interview date
- Findings doc link
- Follow-up actions
- Conversion status

**Daily outreach goal:** 10 lawyers contacted per day until 3 interviews scheduled

**Expected conversion:**
- 10 outreach → 3 responses → 1 scheduled interview
- Need 30 outreach contacts to schedule 3 interviews

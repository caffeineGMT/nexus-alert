# Sales Messaging Extraction Framework

**Purpose:** Extract high-converting sales messaging from immigration lawyer interviews by capturing their exact language, pain points, and desired outcomes.

---

## Messaging Extraction During Interviews

### 1. Pain Point Language

**Listen for phrases like:**
- "The worst part is..."
- "It drives me crazy when..."
- "I waste so much time on..."
- "My clients complain about..."
- "We've lost clients because..."

**Template:**

| Pain Point Theme | Exact Quote | Intensity (1-10) | Frequency | Messaging Hook |
|------------------|-------------|------------------|-----------|----------------|
| Time waste | "[Their exact words]" | [Score] | [How often this happens] | "Stop wasting X hours/week on..." |
| Client frustration | "[Quote]" | | | "Your clients deserve..." |
| Competitive disadvantage | "[Quote]" | | | "While you're manually checking, competitors are..." |
| Revenue loss | "[Quote]" | | | "Every delay costs you..." |

**Example:**
| Pain Point Theme | Exact Quote | Intensity (1-10) | Frequency | Messaging Hook |
|------------------|-------------|------------------|-----------|----------------|
| Time waste | "I'm checking that damn website 5 times a day and finding nothing" | 9/10 | Daily | "Stop checking ttp.cbp.dhs.gov 5x/day" |

---

### 2. Desired Outcome Language

**Listen for phrases like:**
- "I just want to..."
- "It would be amazing if..."
- "My ideal solution would..."
- "All I need is..."
- "Success looks like..."

**Template:**

| Desired Outcome | Exact Quote | Value Metric | Messaging Benefit |
|-----------------|-------------|--------------|-------------------|
| Time savings | "[Quote]" | [X hours/week] | "Get back X hours/week" |
| Client satisfaction | "[Quote]" | [Net Promoter Score impact] | "Happier clients in X days" |
| More capacity | "[Quote]" | [Additional clients you can serve] | "Serve X more clients without hiring" |
| Competitive edge | "[Quote]" | [Win rate increase] | "Book appointments X% faster" |

---

### 3. Value Perception Language

**Listen for how they quantify value:**
- "This would save me..."
- "I'd pay X for..."
- "That's worth at least..."
- "If it saves X hours, that's..."

**Template:**

| Value Statement | Exact Quote | Calculated ROI | Messaging Proof Point |
|-----------------|-------------|----------------|----------------------|
| ROI | "[Quote]" | $X saved / $Y cost = [R]x | "[R]x return on investment" |

## Objection Language

**Listen for hesitations like:**
- "My concern is..."
- "I'm worried about..."
- "What if..."
- "I don't know if..."

**Template:**

| Objection | Exact Quote | Frequency | Counter-Messaging | Proof Point |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|-------------------|-------------|
| Price | "[Quote]" | [X of 3] | [How to reframe] | [ROI calc] |
| Trust/Reliability | "[Quote]" | [X of 3] | [How to build credibility] | [Uptime, testimonials] |
| Setup complexity | "[Quote]" | [X of 3] | [Ease-of-use proof] | [Time to first value] |

### ROI Positioning

**Script:**
"Let me show you the math:

You said you spend [X hours/week] on this at $[Y/hour] billing rate.
That's $[Z/month] in time cost.

NEXUS Alert Pro is $99/month.

That's a [R]x return - you save $[Z-99] every month.

Even if we save you just [X/2] hours, you're still way ahead.

Make sense?"

---

### Objection Handling (Using Their Language)

**Objection: "That's expensive"**
- Response: "I hear you. But you said you're spending [X hours/week] on this. At your billing rate, that's [calculation]. NEXUS Alert pays for itself in [timeframe]."
- Source: [Interview where we validated ROI]

**Objection: "I need to think about it"**
- Response: "[Lawyer name] from [Firm] said the same thing. I gave them a 60-day free trial - no credit card required. By week 2, they found [X] appointments for clients. Want to try the same?"
- Source: [Interview where trial removed friction]

**Objection: "What if it doesn't work?"**
- Response: "[Competitor manual process pain quote]. We're more reliable than that because we check every 2 minutes, 24/7. You can't do that manually. Worst case, cancel after 60 days. Best case, you never manually check again."
- Source: [Interview highlighting reliability concern]
